<b><code>getJobOwnerMailAddresses</code></b>
<blockquote>
Returns a list with the mail addresses of all job owners. This includes primary owners and all secondary owners of the current job.<br/><br/>

<i>This method returns an empty list if no job owner is configured or the <a href="#ownership">Job and Node ownership plugin</a> is missing.</i>
</blockquote>
